Travel Information

San Francisco's iconic red and orange Golden Gate Bridge spans 1.7 miles and connects the northernmost tip of the city with Marin County. Suspension bridges are the main route for Bay Area commuters, but famous landmarks offer panoramic views of the Pacific, Marine, Alcatraz, and San Francisco capes, attracting visitors from all over the world.
