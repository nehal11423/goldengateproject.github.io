The Golden Gate Bridge

The Golden Gate Bridge is a suspension bridge over the Golden Gate, a 1-mile (1.6 km) wide strait that connects San Francisco Bay to the Pacific Ocean. This structure connects the US cities of San Francisco, California, at the northern end of the San Francisco Peninsula, with Marin County and carries both the United States and the United States. National Highway 101 and California State Highway 1 across the strait.
