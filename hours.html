Hours for The Golden Gate Bridge

The Bridge Welcome Center offers products and historical information. The Welcome Center is open daily from 9am to 6pm and is the premier on-site center dedicated to the history of the Golden Gate Bridge. The center welcomes visitors, provides orientation and information services, houses exhibits, provides high-quality memorabilia and commentary, and is the original 12-foot stainless steel bridge "test" used in 1933. "Tower" will also be exhibited. You can also visit the Golden Gate Parks Conservancy online store to get unique gifts and merchandise. Please contact the welcome center at 4154265220 or customerservice@goldengatebridgestore.org.
